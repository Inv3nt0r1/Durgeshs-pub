Welcome to Durgesh’s Pub

Experience the ultimate media server for streaming Movies, Series, Educational Courses, and more! Think of it as your personal Netflix. You can enjoy any movie or show, pause it at any time, and effortlessly resume from where you left off. Our server keeps track of your progress, ensuring a seamless viewing experience.

I've set this up to provide my family and friends with easy access to a vast library of entertainment without the hassle of searching and downloading from the internet. Hosted on my Raspberry Pi 4, the server streams content directly from my external hard drive. I have made sure to make it smart enough to handle errors automatically, but occasional downtimes may occur due to power outages, Raspberry Pi issues, or internet connectivity problems. If you notice the server is down, please let me know.

To get started, click the button below. The background script will verify if the server is running, perform additional checks, and redirect you to the login page. Enjoy your streaming experience!
